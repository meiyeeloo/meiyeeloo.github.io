Improving Success Rate of OffGamers' Sign up

Live Project • eCommerce

Web Design

Introduction

OffGamers is a famous e-commerce site among Malaysian gamers. The business started 20 years ago and extended its service to selling not just gaming-relevant products.

Problem

To adhere to Anti-Money Laundering regulation, only logged-in members with verified mobile and email are allowed to make a purchase on OffGamers. This means that a smooth sign-up flow is very crucial to business success. However, with a sign-up success rate ranging from 49% to 68%, it raised doubts about why potential buyers could not complete their registration.

Research

Our team used Datadog recordings and system data to analyse issues encountered by real users. One major finding was a significant drop occurred when users were prompted to verify their mobile number. Besides that, I also studied all existing steps to register an OffGamers account, as well as the most recent sign-up trends among competitors.

User flows

Before jumped into design, I created a new user flow to streamline the process.

Design

Appling modern styles with OffGamers branding, I redesigned the complete sign-up flow as well as the new design system. Every aspect, from minor button components to full-screen mockups, was clearly explained through prototypes, wireflows, and documentation.

Teamwork

After finalizing the design, I briefed developers and QA on the new design. Additionally, I reviewed the staging environment and conducted meetings with them to refine it for optimal results.

Result

Upon the launch of the new design, there was a noticeable increase in the sign-up rate, ranging from 63% to 82%. Also, the new design system can be readily applied to the next phase of design.
