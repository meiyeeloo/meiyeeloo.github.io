Improving Registration Flow of OCBC Online Account Opening

Live Project • Banking

Responsive Web Design

Introduction

This project aimed to combine and ease the online application process of two main saving accounts of OCBC bank, which were 360 and Boost accounts.

Problem & Needs

The client needed a more user-friendly way for online banking account application, below were the parts taken into account.

  • Breaking down the existing form’s long structure into smaller steps
  • A new design with a modern and minimalistic look

Discover

Understood issues of the existing application form, analysed, and discussed with members how to enhance the user experience.

User Flow

Broke down the long application flow into 5 major steps focusing on from the most general to most sensitive information.

Design

The new form design reserved OCBC’s corporate identity and infused a fresh and spacious look for better desktop and mobile interaction. It also supported application via mobile since it was responsive.

Result

Client was satisfied with the new design and had launched it onsite to prompt new user application.
