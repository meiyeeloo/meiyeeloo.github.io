Revamping OJO Eyewear's Website

Live Project • eCommerce

Web Design

Introduction

OJO Eyewear is Japanese-inspired eyewear brand in more than 80 branches in Malaysia and the Philippines.

Problem

They introduced its online shopping platform in mid of 2021 but realised the need to revamp it to have a more stylish looking and better user experience to be more competitve.
