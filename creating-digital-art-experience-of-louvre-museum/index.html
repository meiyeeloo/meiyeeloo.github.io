Creating Digital Art Experience of Louvre Museum

Self-Project • Art

User Flow

Mobile App Design

Introduction

Since travelling is not allowed during the pandemic lock down, I was inspired to design a digital experience of art exhibition for Louvre Museum.

Problem

  • How to mimic the experience of visiting museum physically with current technology?
  • What made people love visiting Louvre Museum?
  • What gave the museum a poor review?

Discover

Through Google Review, studied what visitors think and feel after a visit to discover existing painpoints and enjoyable experience.

User Flow

Defined a user flow which includes comprehensive user experience and different interactions.

Wireframing

Built low-fidelity wireframes to ensure the structure of every screen supports the user flow.

Design & Prototyping

Created high-fidelity design, prototyped the screen according to the user flow, and tested the result!
