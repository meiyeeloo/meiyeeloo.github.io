Customising a Delivery App for Fine Coffee & Flowers

Self-Project • Food Delivery

User Flow

Prototype Testing

Mobile App Design

Introduction

This was a design challenge for myself to design a food delivery app for a nearby cafe, Fine Coffee and Flowers.

Problem

Knowing that this cafe mainly relies on third party apps such as Grab and Food Panda to gain more business, I began to think what if it has its own exclusive food delivery app to cut cost from paying commission.

Research

Experienced the apps of direct and indirect competitors to explore their strengths, weeknesses, and current design trends in food delivery industry.

Trends
  • Optimised UI
  • Large product image (direct competitors)
Strengths
  • Loyalty program
  • Discounts
  • Provide options for pick up and delivery
Weaknesses
  • Unable to modify the shopping cart (direct competitors)
  • Only 1 out of 4 apps allows in-store order

User Flow

Defined user flow to demostrate a complete ordering cycle from a new user to existing user.

Wireframing, Design & Test

Tested design since working on low-fidelity wireframes to get earlier feedbacks on what did and did not work well. Also, observed testers reaction when interacting with prototypes to know if they were confused on certain flows.

Result

Design was finalised with the complete flow to place an delivery order.
